## About VORNAC

VORNAC GmbH is an elite cybersecurity firm specializing in advanced, deep-scope **penetration testing** and the development of secure, next-generation **Agentic AI systems**. 

We bridge the gap between offensive cybersecurity and cognitive automation. By training and deploying autonomous AI agents to augment defensive workflows and simulate sophisticated threat actors, we ensure enterprise perimeters remain impenetrable.

---

## Our Open Source & Code Posture

> ### **Security by Default.**
> To protect our proprietary AI models, custom exploit frameworks, and client telemetry, VORNAC operates strictly within isolated, highly secure private repositories. Our public footprint is intentionally minimal, reflecting our commitment to absolute operational security (OPSEC).

---

## Core Domains of Expertise

* **Advanced Penetration Testing:** Comprehensive red-teaming, full-chain infrastructure exploitation, and cloud architecture security audits.
* **Agentic AI Engineering:** Training, fine-tuning, and orchestrating autonomous, multi-agent AI networks capable of complex reasoning and execution.
* **AI Red Teaming & Security:** Stress-testing LLMs and AI agent architectures against prompt injection, data poisoning, and autonomous drift.
